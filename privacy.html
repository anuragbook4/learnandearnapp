Privacy Policy for Learn & Earn

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Learn & Earn. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our educational quiz and gamified rewards application.

By using Learn & Earn, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our services, we may collect the following information:

Google Account Information

When you sign in using Google, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Profile Picture
  • Unique User ID

Device Information

We may collect limited device-related information to prevent fraud and ensure platform stability, including:

  • Device Model
  • Operating System Version
  • Device Language
  • Device Identifiers (IP Address, Android ID)
  • App Version Information

Activity & Rewards Information

To operate our educational rewards system and track your game progress, we collect:

  • In-game Coins Earned
  • Quiz/Game Progress & Performance
  • Bonus Rewards & Referral Activity
  • Reward Redemption History
  • Account Activity Logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts.
  • Provide educational quiz content and track game progress.
  • Manage the in-app coin rewards and referral benefits system.
  • Process reward redemption requests (e.g., UPI, digital gift codes, game currencies).
  • Prevent fraud, multiple account creation, cheating, and abuse.
  • Improve application performance and user experience.
  • Respond to customer support requests.
  • Maintain platform security and comply with legal obligations.

3. Reward System & Redemptions

Learn & Earn provides virtual coins and loyalty rewards through eligible in-app entertainment and educational activities, including educational quizzes, math puzzle solving, casual wheel-spin mini-games, virtual scratch cards, and casual symbol-matching mini-games available within the application. Users can redeem these accumulated coins for various rewards such as UPI transfers, digital gift cards, or game gift codes.

Redemption Processing

Approved redemption requests are generally processed within 7 to 14 business days.

In certain circumstances, including but not limited to high transaction volume, technical issues, manual verification reviews, security checks, or third-party payment provider delays, redemptions may take up to 21 days (3 weeks) to complete.

Users acknowledge that reward processing times may vary and are not guaranteed.

Important Notice: All mini-games (including quiz challenges, spins, scratch cards, and symbol-matching games) are entirely free to play and are intended solely for entertainment and gamification purposes. Learn & Earn does not require, accept, or allow any real-money purchases, deposits, or stakes to play these games. Our platform does not offer, promote, or tolerate gambling, betting, or real-money wagering.

4. Fraud Prevention & Account Security

To protect the platform, our partners, and our users, Learn & Earn reserves the right to investigate suspicious activity. The following activities are strictly prohibited and may result in immediate reward cancellation, redemption rejection, temporary suspension, or permanent account termination:

  • Creating or using multiple accounts on a single device.
  • Using VPNs, proxies, fake emulators, or rooting tools to manipulate location or data.
  • Using automated scripts, bots, or auto-clickers.
  • Exploiting bugs, glitches, or system errors to gain unfair advantages or coins.
  • Generating fake or fraudulent referrals.
  • Any activity intended to manipulate the application's rewards system.

All decisions regarding account reviews, suspensions, and reward eligibility remain at the sole discretion of Learn & Earn.

5. Advertisements

Learn & Earn uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob uses user choice models (such as Rewarded Ads) where users can voluntarily choose to watch ads to earn virtual coins. AdMob and its partners may collect information such as:

  • Advertising ID (GAID)
  • Device Information
  • Ad Interaction Data

This data is used to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements. For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy. We strictly follow Google's policies and never force or incentivize users to artificially click on advertisements.

6. Third-Party Services

Our application utilizes trusted third-party services for seamless functionality, including but not limited to:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore / Realtime Database

These third-party services may collect and process information in accordance with their own respective privacy policies.

7. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of electronic transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Learn & Earn is intended for users aged 18 years and above due to the financial nature of reward redemptions. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we discover that personal information from a child under 13 has been collected inadvertently, it will be removed from our servers as soon as reasonably possible.

Upon receiving a valid deletion request, all personal data (including Name, Email, and History) will be permanently deleted from our databases within 30 days, except where certain data must be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or historical record-keeping purposes.

Users agree to use the application at their own discretion and risk.
